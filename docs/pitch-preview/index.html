Legal wording safety
  • Say: risk indicator for developer or legal review; not legal advice.
  • Do not say: compliant, lawsuit-proof, guaranteed secure, automatic violation.
  • FTC: unclear AI disclosures or unsupported claims may warrant review.
  • Apple/Google: data declarations must accurately describe relevant data use.
  • DMCA/UGC: safe harbor has multiple conditions; do not reduce it to a fee.
